Services

Mega Union Global Limited’s services cover the following three broad categories:

  • Sourcing, outsourcing and procurement services
  • Quality control management
  • Logistics

Sourcing, outsourcing and
procurement services

For every engagement Mega Union Global Limited designs a procurement package tailor-made for its clients. Its services are conducted by highly experienced team members who meticulously and exhaustively search and screen the market so that our clients’ expectations and specifications are met and delivered.

With its extensive network of trusted Asian suppliers and manufacturers, Mega Union Global Limited has proudly posted high procurement success rates continuously, and always to the satisfaction of its clients.

Whether you want to focus on business sales and leave your purchasing endeavours to a trusted agent, or you find procurement in Asian countries a challenge for geographical reasons, Mega Union Global Limited is more than willing and eager to assist you.

Quality control management (QCM)

To assure clients that the goods and products procured for them meet their quality standards, Mega Union Global Limited also offers quality tests and test plan services. For medical supplies, Mega Union Global Limited engages the most reputable laboratories to inspect our suppliers’ production processes and verify that their products pass quality and safety standards. Our clients are always assured of the integrity of the medical supplies they acquire through us.

Logistics

Mega Union Global Limited engages the best companies in the forwarding business for shipment of procured products to clients. Our long-standing partnership with these companies has allowed us to experience first-hand the quality of their services, and allows us likewise to work for substantial shipping discounts in our clients’ behalf.

We always leave clients with the option of having their products shipped via sea or air, or of shipping immediately a particular procurement or consolidating several procurements together into a single shipment.

With Mega Union Global Limited you are assured not only of affordable and fast shipping, but also shipping at your full control.
