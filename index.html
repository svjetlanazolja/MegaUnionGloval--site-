ABOUT US

A decade of experience has allowed us to grow into a leading sourcing company

Mega Union Global Limited is a Hong Kong based company offering sourcing and business consultancy services. It focuses especially on sourcing Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and other medical supplies from Asia for its clients worldwide.

Mega Union Global Limited is more than eager to help you with your international business procurement needs, and invites you to experience the same satisfaction that it has given its clients.

Mega Union Global Limited also offers a wide range of common medical supplies enjoying high demand out of its own shelf. Clients may purchase these directly from us, saving valuable time and cutting the procurement process short, while enjoying the assurance of product quality that Mega Union Global Limited is known for.

Covid-19

The COVID-19 crisis has brought about a surge in demand for Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and medical supplies. Most of our suppliers have met these demands at the expected quality.

The market has seen, however, the emergence and proliferation of bogus supplies from unscrupulous sellers. Purchasers have not only lost millions of dollars, but front liners and health workers who lead the global fight against COVID-19 have also had their lives endangered with these poor imitations.

Mega Union Global Limited offers to have medical supplies of assured quality procured for you, as they are becoming more important than ever. With our test plan services, we and our partner-laboratories conduct first-hand inspection of our suppliers to assure you that you will receive only genuine products and medical supplies that are up to the highest safety standards.

FAQ

Our Frequently Asked Questions

Who are the suppliers of Mega Union Global Limited?

Mega Union Global Limited’s suppliers are trusted manufacturers from China and other parts of Asia such as Thailand, Indonesia and the Philippines. We select our suppliers based on stringent criteria, and those we choose to serve our clients are guaranteed to be duly licensed businesses by their respective governments. They are an integral part of our services. We share an efficient and highly complementary working relationship with them, and we vouch for their reliability.

Does Mega Union Global Limited source its supplies from online platforms?

No. Mega Union Global Limited abides by the ethics of the profession that procurement activities be done first-hand by the service provider. Some of our suppliers may sell online, but as far as fulfilling client needs are concerned, Mega Union Global Limited conducts transactions with its suppliers directly. We don’t serve our clients by expediently placing orders for them online.

Can a client engage Mega Union Global Limited to fulfil an agreed contract with a personal supplier, or to engage Mega Union Global Limited to negotiate a contract with a personal supplier?

Mega Union Global Limited agrees to be engaged only on an immediate basis where it receives its clients’ orders directly and commits to fulfil them at the specified terms and conditions. For reasons of practicality and business ethics, it does not agree to be engaged as a third party negotiator between a client and its personal supplier.

Can a customer expect the best prices from Mega Union Global Limited?

Yes, definitely. In fact, negotiating better prices for its customers is the part of its service Mega Union Global Limited is most proud of. Furthermore, Mega Union Global Limited, quite figuratively, fills a clients’ shoes and does all that a diligent client would if he were personally making the purchase, such as to inspect and ensure the quality of the goods. Mega Union Global Limited’s services are very comprehensive that clients can always work for and get the best end of a transaction.

Does Mega Union Global Limited source from manufacturers or from trading companies?

As a matter of policy, Mega Union Global Limited links its clients directly with manufacturers as to ensure that they obtain their goods from the first source and for the lowest prices. It may happen, however, that these goods will have to be obtained from second-layer sellers such trading companies as when, for instance, a manufacturer is unlicensed for export, or when a manufacturer conducts its business only through a trading company. These are rare instances, and the majority of our customers do obtain their goods directly from manufacturers.

Does Mega Union Global Limited receive commissions from its suppliers?

No. The fees that Mega Union Global Limited receives are only those that are set forth in its service contract with clients, which are either set fees or percentage fees based on their closed deals with suppliers. We don’t receive commissions or any other amount based on discreet arrangements with our suppliers. Transparency, for us, is a corporate value.

Can Mega Union Global Limited gather various product samples from different suppliers in behalf of its clients?

Yes of course, and this is part of our service meant to ensure that the client obtains the products he needs at the exact specification. In addition, and as previously mentioned, we conduct on-site manufacturing process inspection of our suppliers as to ensure that we gather only the highest grade and choicest samples for our clients to compare and choose from.

What is the minimum engagement period for a full-service sourcing?

Mega Union Global Limited sets a minimum engagement period of three (3) months for full-service sourcing. Sourcing from mainland China could get quite complicated and fulfilling a service contract could require a host of other activities from Mega Union Global Limited such as the engagement of third parties for some specific tasks. Hence the period.

Can a client pay the supplier through Mega Union Global Limited?

Sales transactions completed through Mega Union Global Limited’s procurement services are normally between our clients and our suppliers, hence all payments must also be direct between them. However, Mega Union Global Limited may accommodate payment in behalf of its clients, as when it is assigned as the client’s exclusive agent. Accommodations of this kind come with a nominal fee.

Will there be hidden costs for a client aside from the service fees set forth and charged in the service procurement contract?

While Mega Union Global Limited wishes to keep costs for its clients to the minimum, there will always be unanticipated expenses, fees and charges as procurement services are performed. Mega Union Global Limited will bear these expenses momentarily, but they will be charged to the client in the final accounting of the service contract.
