Approaches

What does sourcing and outsourcing mean?

Sourcing, also known as procurement, is the business practice of locating and selecting suppliers for sellers based on stringent standards or criteria.

Outsourcing, on the other hand, is the practice of hiring or contracting third parties to create goods that are normally produced in-house.

Businesses often resort to sourcing and outsourcing as a cost-saving measure. Cost efficiency is a critical factor for business success, and companies that are able to adopt means or design systems that translate to savings enjoy tremendous competitive edge over their more traditional and conservative counterparts.
